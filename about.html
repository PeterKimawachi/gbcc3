About Us

Our Mission

To provide a safe, loving, and nurturing environment for vulnerable children, empowering them to reach their full potential through education, healthcare, and spiritual guidance.

Our Vision

A world where every child has access to love, care, and opportunities to thrive, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our History

Gospel Believer's Children Center was founded in 2010 by a group of dedicated individuals who saw the need to support vulnerable children in our community. Over the years, we have grown from a small shelter to a fully-fledged children's center, providing holistic care to over 200 children annually.

Our journey has been marked by challenges and triumphs, but through it all, our commitment to the children has remained unwavering. We are proud of the impact we have made and are excited about the future as we continue to expand our reach and services.
